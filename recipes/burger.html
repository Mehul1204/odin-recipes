Burger

Description :

To make a burger take a burger bun and slice it in half

Apply the necessary sauces and patty and your done

Ingredients :

  • Burger buns
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Patty

Steps :

  1. Slice bun in half
  2. Apply sauce
  3. Place the patty
  4. Close with the buns
